Our Mission

Drishya Welfare Society is committed to uplifting underprivileged communities through education, healthcare, women empowerment, and sustainable social programs.

Our Programs

We work across key areas to uplift and empower underprivileged communities.

Education

Providing quality education, school support, and learning resources to children from marginalized communities.

Healthcare

Organizing medical camps, health awareness programs, and access to essential healthcare services.

Women Empowerment

Supporting women through skill development, self-help groups, and livelihood opportunities.
