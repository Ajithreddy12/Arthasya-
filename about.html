About Us

Committed to creating positive change in society

Who We Are

Drishya Welfare Society is a non-profit organization dedicated to empowering underprivileged communities through education, healthcare, women empowerment, and social welfare initiatives.

Our Mission

To uplift communities by providing access to education, healthcare, and sustainable development programs that create long-term impact.

Our Vision

A society where every individual has equal opportunities to live with dignity, independence, and hope.

Our Core Values

  • 🤝 Integrity & Transparency
  • ❤️ Compassion & Inclusion
  • 📘 Education for All
  • 🌱 Sustainable Development
  • 👩‍👩‍👧 Empowering Communities
